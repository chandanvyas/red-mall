PROMOTERS : RED Mall Ghaziabad

RED MALL is an ambitious project being promoted by R.E.D Solutions Pvt. Ltd. and developed by Celebration City Project Pvt. Ltd. In Ghaziabad. R.E.D Solutions Pvt. Ltd. has been founded with a vision to bring world-class shopping and entertainment experience for customers, all under one roof. This company has been established by industry veterans -Mr Rakesh Babbar and Mr Sanjeev Bewtra, who have a vast experience in the real estate industry and in-depth understanding of the business of family entertainment.

The ideation of an end-to-end family entertainment destination is the brain child of Mr Rakesh Babbar, who has a rich experience of more than 15 years in the field of theme parks. He was a strong pillar behind the success of Appu Ghar in Pragati Maidan, which received a footfall of around 1.4 million in a year. He has also been instrumental in the successful development and operations of Entertainment Destinations like- The Great India Place and Worlds of Wonder in Noida, and Metro Walk and Adventure Island in Rohini. Currently, he is also actively involved in the strategy and development of Appu Ghar amusement parks in Jaipur and Gurgaon.

After a successful stint in the electronics industry, Mr Sanjeev Bewtra became a part of IAL (International Amusement Limited) as the Director of Sales and Marketing.
